Privacy Policy for Ushould2

Last updated: July 20, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Ushould2. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our media tracking application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Profile picture (optional)
  • User Content: Media logs, ratings, reviews, and social posts
  • Contact Information: Access to contacts (only with your permission) for finding friends

Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and navigation patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes to improve stability
  • Analytics Data: App performance metrics and feature usage statistics
  • Advertising Data: Ad impressions and interactions (for free version users)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Enable account creation, media tracking, and social features
  • Personalization: Customize recommendations and improve your experience
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the app to make improvements
  • Communication: Send important updates about your account or service changes
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads in the free version of the app
  • Safety & Security: Protect against fraud and ensure app security

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement display in the free version
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management for premium features
  • TMDB, Google Books, BoardGameGeek: Media information and images

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share
  • For Social Features: Your public activity is visible to friends you connect with
  • Service Providers: With third-party services necessary to operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase's encrypted cloud services
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Account access is protected by authentication credentials
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Update: Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads or upgrade to premium to remove ads
  • Data Portability: Export your media logs and ratings

Children's Privacy

Ushould2 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
